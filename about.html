Our Foundation: Integrity and Excellence

Our Mission

To be Sweden's most trusted and compliant new entrant in the tobacco wholesale sector. We are dedicated to providing the market with high-quality products sourced from reputable European manufacturing partners, while operating with complete transparency and unwavering adherence to the law.

Our Vision

To build a sustainable business based on strong, long-term relationships with our supply, distribution, and retail partners, creating shared value through a reliable and responsible business model.

Our Core Values

  • Compliance: Upholding every aspect of Swedish and EU tobacco regulation is the cornerstone of our operation.
  • Quality: We partner exclusively with manufacturers who meet the highest standards of production and quality control.
  • Partnership: Our success is built on mutual trust and collaboration with our suppliers, distributors, and retail clients.
  • Responsibility: We are committed to conducting our business in a responsible and transparent manner.
