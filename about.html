Our Foundation: Integrity and Excellence

Our Mission

To be Sweden's most trusted and compliant new entrant in the tobacco wholesale sector. We are dedicated to providing the market with high-quality products sourced from reputable European manufacturing partners, while operating with complete transparency and unwavering adherence to the law.

Our Vision

To build a sustainable business based on strong, long-term relationships with our supply, distribution, and retail partners, creating shared value through a reliable and responsible business model.

Our Core Values

  • Compliance: Upholding every aspect of Swedish and EU tobacco regulation is the cornerstone of our operation.
  • Quality: We partner exclusively with manufacturers who meet the highest standards of production and quality control.
  • Partnership: Our success is built on mutual trust and collaboration with our suppliers, distributors, and retail clients.
  • Responsibility: We are committed to conducting our business in a responsible and transparent manner.

Company Structure

HatakeSmoke operates as Nordic Tobacco AB, a Swedish private limited liability company (Aktiebolag). This corporate structure provides the necessary legal framework for our wholesale tobacco operations while ensuring full compliance with Swedish corporate law and limiting shareholder liability to invested capital.

Business Model

Our business model centers on wholesale distribution of private-label tobacco products sourced from established European manufacturers. We focus on building long-term partnerships with OEM suppliers across the EU, ensuring consistent quality and supply chain reliability. Our target launch date is February 2026, allowing sufficient time for all regulatory approvals and compliance frameworks to be established.

Regulatory Excellence

We maintain the highest standards of regulatory compliance through our comprehensive framework that includes municipal wholesale permits, Approved Stockist status with Skatteverket, and full integration with the EU Track & Trace system. Our self-monitoring program ensures continuous compliance with all Swedish and EU tobacco regulations, from product notifications to packaging requirements.
