An Investment in a Strategic and Compliant Business Model

The Market Opportunity

The Swedish tobacco market presents a significant opportunity for a new, agile, and fully compliant player. With our strategic positioning as a quality value alternative and comprehensive regulatory framework, HatakeSmoke is positioned to capture market share through superior compliance, competitive pricing, and reliable supply chain management. Our February 2026 launch timeline allows us to enter the market with a fully established operational infrastructure.

Financial Structure & Investment Requirements

Nordic Tobacco AB operates with a lean but professional organizational structure designed for efficiency and scalability. Our key operational positions include a Compliance & Logistics Manager (SEK 800,000 annually) and a Key Account Manager (SEK 600,000 base annually), plus social security contributions of approximately 31.42% of gross salaries. The minimum share capital requirement is SEK 25,000, with additional working capital needs determined by market entry scale and inventory requirements.
