Building Success Together

A Partnership Based on Quality and Trust

HatakeSmoke seeks to build long-term relationships with EU-based OEM manufacturers. Outline the key criteria for partnership:

  • Demonstrated expertise in EU TPD and Swedish packaging regulations.
  • Certified capability to provide complete and accurate data for EU-CEG product notifications.
  • Full integration with the EU Track & Trace system.
  • A transparent and reputable corporate history.

The HatakeSmoke Commercial Proposition

Our value proposition for distributors and retailers is built on three fundamental pillars that ensure mutual success and sustainable growth in the Swedish tobacco market.

  • A Competitive Commercial Offer: Our products are structured to provide a superior margin for our retail partners while maintaining competitive end-consumer pricing.
  • Guaranteed Compliance: We assume all regulatory responsibility, delivering products that are 100% compliant with Swedish and EU regulations and ready for immediate sale.
  • Reliable Supply: Through our diversified network of European manufacturers and established distribution channels, we guarantee a consistent and dependable supply chain with minimal disruption risk.

Partnership Opportunities

We are actively seeking partnerships with qualified distributors and retailers who share our commitment to quality and compliance. Our partnership model is designed to create long-term value for all stakeholders while maintaining the highest standards of business ethics and regulatory adherence.
